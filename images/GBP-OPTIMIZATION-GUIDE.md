## PART 3: PHOTO STRATEGY

Photos are one of the most underutilized GBP ranking factors. Google says businesses with photos get **42% more direction requests** and **35% more website clicks**.

### Photo Categories to Upload
1. **Logo** — transparent background, high resolution
2. **Cover photo** — truck with crew, or before/after garage
3. **Team photos** — faces build trust
4. **Before & After** — the most clicked GBP photos for junk removal
5. **Truck/Equipment** — show your professional setup
6. **Job site photos** — garages, estates, dumpsters being filled
7. **Certificate of Insurance** — builds trust for commercial clients

### Photo Upload Schedule
- Upload **2-3 new photos per week minimum**
- Google rewards fresh, consistent photo activity
- Use geotagged photos (embed GPS coordinates using tools like GeoSetter or Pic2Map)
- Name your files descriptively before uploading: `junk-removal-naperville-il-cleanout-guys.jpg`

## PART 4: REVIEWS — YOUR #1 RANKING WEAPON

Based on @IsellSaaSGuy's strategy: **Reviews are Bitcoin for local SEO.**

### The Goal
- Get to **50+ reviews** as fast as possible to establish authority
- Maintain a **4.8+ star average**
- Respond to **every single review** within 24 hours

### How to Get Reviews Fast
1. **After every completed job:** Text the customer:
   > "Hi [Name]! Thank you for choosing Cleanout Guys. We'd love your honest feedback — it takes 30 seconds and helps us a ton: [Google Review Link]"

2. **Direct Google review link:** Get yours from:
   Google Maps → Your Business → Get More Reviews → Share Review Form

3. **Email follow-up:** Send a follow-up email 24 hours after the job

4. **Business card:** Print cards with QR code directly to your Google review page

5. **Invoice footer:** Add "Love our service? Leave us a Google review!" with QR code

### Responding to Reviews
**Positive review template:**
> "Thank you so much, [Name]! We're so glad our team was able to help with your [service] in [city]. We appreciate you taking the time to leave a review — it means the world to our team. We're always here if you need us again! — The Cleanout Guys Team"

**Negative review template:**
> "Hi [Name], thank you for your feedback. We're sorry your experience didn't meet our standards. Please call us at (847) 306-9567 so we can make this right for you. We stand behind every job 100%."

### Review Automation Tool
Consider: **ReviewRover** (promoted by @IsellSaaSGuy) or alternatives:
- Birdeye
- Podium
- NiceJob
- Grade.us

These tools automatically send review requests via SMS after each job closes.
