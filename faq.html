💲 Pricing & Quotes

How much does junk removal cost in Chicago?+
Junk removal in Chicago and suburbs typically ranges from $100 to $600+ depending on the volume, type of items, and ease of access. Cleanout Guys charges by truckload volume with 100% upfront, flat pricing. We never charge by the hour or add mystery fees. Our pricing includes all labor, loading, hauling, and disposal. Call (847) 306-9567 or submit our online form for a free, no-obligation quote.
Do you provide free quotes?+
Yes — our quotes are always 100% free and no-obligation. We can provide an estimate over the phone based on your description, or we'll send a crew to do a free on-site assessment. The price you agree to before we start is the exact amount you pay. No surprises, ever.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Venmo, Zelle, and personal checks. Payment is collected after the job is completed and you're satisfied.
Are there any hidden fees?+
Absolutely not. The price we quote is the price you pay — period. Our fees cover all labor, fuel, loading, and disposal. The only scenario where a quote may change is if you add additional items beyond what was quoted, which we'll always discuss with you in advance before proceeding.

📅 Scheduling & Service

Do you offer same-day junk removal in Chicago?+
Yes! We offer same-day junk removal throughout Chicago and most suburbs. Call (847) 306-9567 before noon and we'll do our best to dispatch a crew that same day, subject to availability. We also offer scheduled appointments for weekdays, Saturdays, and limited Sunday availability.
What are your hours of operation?+
We're available:
Monday–Friday: 7:00am – 7:00pm
Saturday: 7:00am – 5:00pm
Sunday: 8:00am – 3:00pm

For urgent situations, call us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
How long does a typical junk removal job take?+
Most standard junk removal jobs take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on volume and access. A single-item pickup might take 20 minutes. A full garage cleanout typically takes 1–3 hours. A full estate or hoarding cleanout can take a full day or multiple days — we'll provide an accurate time estimate when we quote your job.
How do I prepare for my junk removal appointment?+
Very little preparation is needed. Simply:
• Identify the items you want removed
• Ensure our crew can access the area (unlock gates, garages, etc.)
• Separate anything you want to keep from what we're hauling away
• You do NOT need to bring items outside — we go inside garages, basements, attics, and anywhere else.

📦 What We Accept

What items do you remove?+
We remove almost everything, including:
• Furniture (couches, beds, dressers, tables, chairs)
• Appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, microwaves, dishwashers)
• Mattresses & box springs
• Electronics (TVs, computers, printers)
• Yard waste & outdoor furniture
• Construction debris (drywall, lumber, concrete, roofing)
• Exercise equipment
• Hot tubs & trampolines
• Office equipment
• General household clutter & boxes
What items do you NOT accept?+
For safety and legal reasons, we do NOT accept:
• Paint, stains, or solvents
• Household chemicals or cleaners
• Asbestos-containing materials
• Propane tanks (full or empty)
• Motor oil, gasoline, or fuels
• Medical waste or sharps
• Radioactive materials
• Fluorescent bulbs (contact a local recycling center)

If you're unsure about a specific item, just call us at (847) 306-9567 and we'll let you know.
Do you remove single items or do you have a minimum?+
Yes, we remove single items! There is a minimum charge that covers our crew's time and travel, but we're happy to come out for a single couch, refrigerator, mattress, or any other item. Call us and we'll quote it for you.

🌿 Eco-Friendly & Donations

Do you donate or recycle removed items?+
Absolutely. Garage Cleanout Guys is committed to responsible, eco-friendly disposal. Our process:
1. We first assess items for donation to Chicago-area charities and nonprofits
2. Recyclable materials go to appropriate recycling centers
3. Only items that truly cannot be reused or recycled go to licensed disposal facilities

We aim to divert 60% or more of all collected material away from landfills on every job.
Can I get a receipt for donated items for tax purposes?+
We can provide a general description of donated items, but official tax receipts must come from the receiving charity. We're happy to share which local Chicago organizations we partner with so you can follow up with them directly for documentation.
