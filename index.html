Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to the questions we hear most from Chicago-area customers.

How much does junk removal cost in Chicago? +
Junk removal in Chicago typically runs between $100–$600 depending on volume, item types, and access. Cleanout Guys charges by the truckload with completely transparent, upfront pricing. We offer free on-site quotes with no obligation — the price we give you is exactly what you pay. Call (847) 306-9567 for a free estimate.
Do you offer same-day junk removal in Chicago? +
Yes! We offer same-day junk removal throughout Chicago and most suburbs. Call us before noon at (847) 306-9567 and we'll do our best to dispatch a crew the same day, subject to availability. We also offer next-day and scheduled appointments.
What items do you take? +
We remove almost everything: furniture, appliances, mattresses, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, office equipment, hot tubs, exercise equipment, tools, and general household junk. We do not accept hazardous materials including paint, chemicals, asbestos, propane tanks, or medical waste.
Are you licensed and insured in Illinois? +
Absolutely. Garage Cleanout Guys is fully licensed and insured in the state of Illinois. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and worker's compensation coverage on every single job. You'll never have to worry about property damage or on-site injuries.
Do you donate or recycle items instead of sending to the landfill? +
Yes — we're committed to responsible, eco-friendly disposal. Before any item goes to a landfill, our crew sorts through for items that can be donated to Chicago-area charities or taken to recycling centers. We aim to divert at least 60% of collected materials away from landfills. It's good for the environment and good for the community.
How do I prepare for junk removal? +
Very little prep is needed. Simply have a clear idea of what you want removed and make sure our crew can access the area. You don't need to move items to the curb — we go inside garages, attics, basements, and anywhere else. Just point and we'll haul it away.
